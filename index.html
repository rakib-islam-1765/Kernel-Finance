Kernel Launchpad

The Kernel Launchpad or IDO platform is a
dedicated platform that allows founders of
new cryptocurrency projects to launch their
crypto assets across several blockchain
networks, including Binance Smart Chain,
Ethereum blockchain, Solana blockchain
and Matic blockchain

Kernelswap

Kernelswap is our Automated Market Maker
(AMM) that allows you to trade a wide range
of cryptocurrencies in a decentralized
ecosystem. The Kernelswap AMM also enables
liquidity provision and farming.

networks
